Rasau City Project

Integrated Urban Rasau Jaya Mandiri , Kubu Raya District , West Kalimantan, in the 1960s , Rasau Jaya is known as one of the base placement of migrants from Java . Rasau Jaya which is about 30 kilometers from the city of Pontianak , Rasau City property development located in a 40 hectare of land and only 5 minutes from Supadio Airport International this location area now becoming one of the integrated agribusiness development center. One of the districts in Kubu Raya was also the backbone of the transport of water between regions due to the presence of water port . As one of the most common types of homes available, two bedroom spaces give just enough space for efficiency yet offer more comfort than a smaller one bedroom or studio. In this post, we'll show some of our favorite two bedroom house plans all shown in beautiful 3D perspective. Sada Pro plan to develop 2600 Landed House, in 3 phase project development frame. The Projects is expected to be completed in early 2016.
